Steak Recipe

Steak is a popular dish made from beef cut into thin slices. Here's a simple recipe to prepare a delicious steak at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Season both sides of the steaks generously with salt and black pepper.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add olive oil and garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the steaks to the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare doneness.
  4. Add butter and thyme to the skillet and baste the steaks with the melted butter for 1 minute.
  5. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
