Brisket Recipe

Brisket is a tough cut of beef that becomes tender and delicious when slow-cooked. Here's a simple recipe to prepare a mouth-watering brisket at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole beef brisket (about 4-5 lbs)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Rub the brisket with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add olive oil and sear the brisket on all sides until browned.
  4. Add beef broth to the skillet and cover with foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 3-4 hours or until the meat is fork-tender.
  6. If desired, brush with barbecue sauce during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
